Frequently Asked Questions

How big is the igloo?

The igloo has a diameter of 3.6m and a height of 7'2". We require a flat space of at least 4m² to set up.

What areas do you cover?

We cover most of Lancashire. Contact us to check if your location is covered!

Do you need outside power?

Yes, we do. We provide an extension cable for all electrical items needed.
